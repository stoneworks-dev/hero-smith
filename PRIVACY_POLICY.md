# Privacy Policy for Hero Smith

**Last Updated:** February 2, 2026

## Overview

Hero Smith ("the App") is a mobile application for creating and managing heroes for the Draw Steel tabletop role-playing game system. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device. Specifically:

- **No account required** - You can use the App without creating an account or providing any personal information.
- **No analytics** - We do not use any analytics services to track your usage.
- **No advertising** - The App contains no advertisements and no ad-tracking.
- **No network transmission** - Your hero data never leaves your device if you don't share it.

## Data Storage

All hero data, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using a SQLite database. This data:

- Remains entirely on your device
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Is not backed up to any cloud service by the App
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the App

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new policy here and update the "Last Updated" date above.
